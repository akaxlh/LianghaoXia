I will join Data Intelligence Lab@HKU as a postdoctoral fellow, under the supervision of Professor Chao Huang. My research is mainly focused on data mining using deep neural architectures, on which I have published 9 first-authored papers in top-tier conferences and journals. I received my PhD degree in computer science at South China University of Technology (SCUT), where I won He-Jingtang Innovation Prize in 2021. The prize is only awarded to two PhD students university-wide.

Research Interests

  • Data Mining, Deep Neural Networks;
  • Graph Neural Networks, Multiplex Graph Learning;
  • User Preference Modeling, Recommender Systems;
  • Spatial-Temporal Data Mining, Urban Computing.
