Lianghao Xia (夏良昊)

Postdoctoral Fellow, University of Hong Kong

Email GitHub Google Scholar

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Data Intelligence Lab@HKU, under the supervision of Professor Chao Huang. My research is mainly focused on graph-structured data mining using deep neural architectures, on which I have published 10 first-authored papers in top-tier conferences and journals. I received my PhD degree in computer science at South China University of Technology (SCUT) under the supervision of Professor Yong Xu, where I won He-Jingtang Innovation Prize in 2021. The prize is only awarded to two PhD candidates university-wide.

Research Interests

  • Data Mining, Deep Neural Networks;
  • Graph Representation Learning, Self-Supervised Learning;
  • User Preference Modeling, Recommender Systems;
  • Spatial-Temporal Data Mining, Urban Computing.
